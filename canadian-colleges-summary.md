#### Requirements Summary

**Academic Requirements:**
- ✓ High school graduation
- ✓ Competitive GPA (mid to high 80s)
- ✓ English (ENG4U or equivalent)
- ✓ Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U)
- ✓ Two of: Biology (SBI4U), Chemistry (SCH4U), Physics (SPH4U)
- ✓ Prerequisites vary by specific second-year program

**Test Requirements:**
- ✓ English proficiency: IELTS 6.5 / TOEFL 100 (if international)
- ✓ No MCAT required for undergraduate programs

**Document Requirements:**
- ✓ High school transcripts (submitted to OUAC)
- ✓ English language test scores (if required)
- ✓ No supplementary application for Life Sciences category

#### Important Notes about Life Sciences Category
- ⚠️ Life Sciences is an admission CATEGORY, not a specific program
- ⚠️ First year is exploratory - choose specific program in second year
- ⚠️ Many second-year programs require specific high school prerequisites
- ⚠️ Psychology requires Biology; Biochemistry requires Bio and Chemistry
- ⚠️ Review program-specific prerequisites before choosing courses
